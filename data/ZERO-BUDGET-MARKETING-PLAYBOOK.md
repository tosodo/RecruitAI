## 🏛️ EDWARD BERNAYS — The Opinion Leader Strategy

### Core Principle
**"The public doesn't buy products. They buy feelings. Make AI adoption feel inevitable."**

### Bernays' Move: The Opinion Leader Programme

You don't need a budget. You need **3 voices** who look like they're speaking from experience:

**Voice 1 — The Skeptic Turned Believer**
> "I thought AI was hype. Then I automated my admin for a week and saved 14 hours. I'm not a tech person. But now I can't imagine going back."

**Voice 2 — The Time-Starved Professional**  
> "I was working 60-hour weeks chasing leads manually. One AI automation later and I'm sleeping by 10pm. Revenue up 30%. My wife noticed before I did."

**Voice 3 — The Quiet Operator**
> "I run a 3-person business. We don't have a marketing team. We have an AI agent that handles our entire outbound. It's like having a 4th person who never sleeps."

**Where to plant these:**
- LinkedIn posts (not ads — organic)
- WhatsApp group (this is your dinner table)
- One-pager PDF you hand to prospects

**Bernays' Tactic — Create a Ceremony**
> AI adoption is the new thing to do. Make it feel ceremonial. The "AI Audit" isn't a meeting — it's a ritual. You're not selling. You're initiating.

## 🎭 RORY SUTHERLAND — The Context Changer

### Core Principle
**"Stop selling time. Sell the feeling that comes from having time."**

### Sutherland's Move: Reposition, Don't Discount

**Your problem:** "£50/month feels expensive to a small business owner on a tight budget."

**Sutherland's answer:** Don't talk about price. Talk about what the price buys.

❌ "AI automation for £50/month"
✅ "Reclaim your weekend for less than a weekday coffee habit"

❌ "AI chatbot for your website"
✅ "A receptionist who never sleeps, never takes a day off, never forgets a lead"

❌ "Automate your admin"
✅ "Be the business owner who's always responsive but never exhausted"

**The Sutherland Test Question:**
> "If your prospect had exactly ONE more free day per week — what would they do with it? Find out. Then show them your AI is the only way they're getting that day back."

**The Free Audit — Sutherland's Way:**
> Don't call it a "consultation." Call it a "Time Audit." Give them 30 minutes. Give them a number. "You're losing £1,240/month to manual admin. Here's how to get it back in 48 hours." Free doesn't feel like charity. It feels like a transaction.

**Sutherland's Unfair Advantage:**
> Personal reputation beats corporate branding every time. Osodo. T's name on this is a feature, not a risk. "Built by a real person who uses this himself." That's the message.

## 📈 ERIC SIU — The Compound Growth Engine

### Core Principle
**"One percent better every day. Content is the foundation. Build in public."**

### Eric Siu's Move: The Content Flywheel

**Zero budget. But you have time, opinions, and proximity to the work.**

**The Flywheel (in order of leverage):**

**1. LinkedIn — Daily for 30 days (No Budget Required)**
> Day 1: "I helped a UK estate agent save 11 hours a week using AI. Here's exactly what we did."
> Day 3: "The average small business owner spends 6 hours/week on admin they hate. What if you got all of it back?"
> Day 7: "We just hit £500 MRR. Here's what worked and what didn't." (build in public)
> Day 14: "I built an AI agent named Sasha. She alerts me when a lead goes cold." (content IS the product)

**Rule:** Post 3x/week minimum. Same day each week. Compound effect takes 60 days.

**2. The Lead Magnet PDF (Eric's Direct Response Move)**
> "The 10-Minute AI Audit — Find Your Hidden £1,000"
> One page. Interactive. Shows them exactly where they're losing money.
> Gate it behind your Calendly. Every download = qualified lead.

**3. The Case Study — Your First Client (Eric's Proof Play)**
> Document everything. Before/after. Hours saved. Revenue recovered.
> This is your sales weapon. Not a PDF — a story.
